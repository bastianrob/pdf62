Welcome to PDF62, your secure and independent solution for managing PDF documents. If you've ever felt uneasy about uploading sensitive financial, medical, or legal documents to random web services, PDF62 was built for you.

## Why We Built PDF62

Many online PDF tools require you to upload your files to their servers. This poses a massive privacy risk:
1. You have no idea where your data is stored.
2. You don't know who has access to it.
3. You rely on an internet connection to process your documents.

We wanted to change that. PDF62 is designed from the ground up to be **100% private and secure**.

## How It Works: The Magic of WebAssembly

We built PDF62 using WebAssembly (Wasm). This means that all of our PDF processing tools—compressing, merging, splitting, and extracting—run completely inside your web browser.

- **Zero Uploads:** Your files never leave your device.
- **Lightning Fast:** Because we don't upload or download massive files over the internet, everything happens almost instantly.
- **Free to Use:** The core tools are free, supported securely through non-intrusive ads and optional donations.

## What Can You Do With PDF62?

We currently offer four core tools to help you manage your PDFs:
- **Compress PDF:** Shrink large files so they're easy to email and share.
- **Merge PDF:** Combine multiple files into a single, cohesive document.
- **Split PDF:** Break apart massive documents into smaller, manageable chunks.
- **Extract Pages:** Pull only the specific pages you need out of a larger file.

## Independent and Ad-Supported

We are independent software engineers. This means we aren't backed by massive venture capital or giant tech corporations. PDF62 is free to use and provided "as is". We support the hosting and development through ads, and we also happily accept donations from users who find our tools valuable.
